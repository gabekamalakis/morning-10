Welcome to LitterLog!

A group of University of Maryland students are exploring a new way to make litter tracking more efficient and visualize areas around Pringe George's County where litter is often found.


LitterLog is a web and smartphone application used to track trash collection during clean-up events in Prince George’s County.


Information submitted through LitterLog including bags of litter removed, items collected and litter pick-up locations will assist the Department of Energy meet their trash reduction rate of 170,628 pounds per year within the Anacostia River Watershed. Data provided will also help the DoE develop real-time maps cataloging litter collected throughout PG County.


How to use LitterLog:

  1. Check where in the community litter has been picked up
  2. Fill out a form for any litter you pick up
  3. Have fun!
