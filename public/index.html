Welcome to LitterLog!

A of University of Maryland students are exploring a new way to make litter tracking more efficient and visualize areas around Pringe George's County litter is often found.


LitterLog is a web and smartphone application used to track trash collection during clean-up events in Prince Georgeâ€™s County.


Information submitted through LitterLog including bags of litter removed, items collected and litter pick-up locations will assist the Department of Energy meet their trash reduction rate of 170,628 pounds per year within the Anacostia River Watershed. Data provided will also help the DoE develop real-time maps cataloging litter collected throughout PG County.

LitterLog is a site developed by University of Maryland students: Gabe Kamalakis, Andrew Garcia, Sarwat Kazmi, Brigidanna Crews, and Adam Fiergang.
